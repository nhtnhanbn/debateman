Instructions for offline use

Email Nhan to report any bugs.

Simple timers for Australasian 3v3 debates. No separate timers for reply speeches, but you can just reset the speaking times and reuse timers.


Timekeepers: Play the bell sound before starting to ensure that debaters are able to hear and are familiar with it.

Speakers: Mute the bell sound by pressing the loudspeaker icon. Test it before starting to ensure that it is muted.




Set speaking times in minutes : seconds. Best to set this beforehand with warning bell before time bell before continuous bell.
